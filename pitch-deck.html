07 / Strategy

The Marlowe Strategy Flywheel

We operate a three-tiered system to harvest alpha from "Soft" on-chain markets. Each tier reinforces the others. Market Making generates the data layer. Intelligence converts that data into directional edge. Funding Arbitrage ensures capital never sits idle. The result is a compounding, self-reinforcing system that grows more efficient at scale.

Tier 1 - Market Making (The Base)

24/7 liquidity on Polymarket and Hyperliquid, capturing the spread paid by retail volume.

Tier 2 - Intelligence and Momentum (The Alpha)

Prediction Markets as Decentralized Oracles; MM data acts as a sensor network, moving RWA positions before traditional markets react.

Tier 3 - Funding Arbitrage (The Stabilizer)

Delta-Neutral Funding Arb harvests yield, keeping capital productive during sideways price action.

Market Making as a High-Fidelity Data Sensor

Our engine detects sentiment shifts in Prediction Markets in real-time. When we see the "Truth Machine" (PM) start to re-price a global event, we don't just collect the spread; we automatically skew our Hyperliquid RWA (Gold/Oil/Equities) quotes and take proprietary momentum positions.
The Shaper's Edge (The Strategic Moat)
We are building the "Data Lake" that the big institutions will eventually crave. By the time they arrive, Marlowe Analytics will have years of proprietary correlation data between prediction odds and asset volatility—positioning us as the inevitable liquidity partner for the institutional wave.
